Our Heritage

The Magga Story

"Every saree carries the patience of a weaver's hands and the soul of Molakalmur."

Devarakondi Silks was born from a deep reverence for Karnataka's handloom legacy. We own and operate a traditional magga (handloom unit) in Molakalmur, Chitradurga — one of the few families still preserving pit-loom silk weaving in its purest form.

Based in Mysore, we connect master weavers directly with saree lovers across India. No intermediaries. No compromises. Just silk woven the way it has been for generations — with artistry, patience, and pride.
